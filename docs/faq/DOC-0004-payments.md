# DOC-0004: FAQ - Customer Payments

**Topic**: Handling Money Issues.

## Q: Customer scanned QR, money left their account, but POS says "Unpaid".
**A:** This is a "Bank Delay".
1.  Ask to see the **Slip** on the customer's phone.
2.  Verify the **Date/Time** match right now.
3.  Verify the **Merchant Name** is "TanJai".
4.  If correct, override the payment on POS:
    - Tap **Pay** > **Other** > **Manual QR Confirm**.
    - Note the "Ref No." from the slip in the notes.

## Q: Can we split the bill?
**A:** Yes.
1.  Tap **Split Bill** on the payment screen.
2.  Select **By Item** (Assign dishes to seats) or **Equal Split** (Divide total by heads).
3.  Process each payment separately.

## Q: Customer wants a VAT Invoice (Tax Invoice).
**A:**
1.  Tap **Tax Invoice** button at Checkout.
2.  Enter Customer Tax ID (or scan their card).
3.  The receipt printer will print the Full Tax Invoice + Copy.

## Q: I charged the wrong amount!
**A:**
- If the payment *has not* gone through: Cancel and re-enter.
- If payment *succeeded*: You must **Refund** the full amount and re-charge. (See DOC-0001).
