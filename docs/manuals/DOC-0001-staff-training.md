# DOC-0001: Staff Training Manual

**Role**: Front of House & Kitchen Staff
**System**: TanJai POS (v2.0)

## 1. The Kitchen Display System (KDS)

### How to Monitor Orders
The KDS screen is divided into 3 zones:
- **WAITING (Left)**: New orders. Tap anywhere on the ticket to see details (e.g., "No Spicy").
- **COOKING (Middle)**: Items currently being made.
- **DONE (Right)**: Items serving now.

### Actions
- **Start Cooking**: Double-tap a ticket in "WAITING" to move it to "COOKING".
- **Serve**: Double-tap a ticket in "COOKING" to move it to "DONE".
- **Undo**: Made a mistake? Long-press any ticket to see the "Undo" option.

## 2. Managing Orders (Front of House)

### Voiding an Item
1. Go to **Table Map** > Select Table.
2. Swipe left on the item you want to cancel.
3. Tap **Void**.
4. Enter **Manager PIN** (Default: 1234) to confirm.

### Processing Refunds
*Note: Refunds can only be processed for orders paid within the last 24 hours.*
1. Go to **History** menu.
2. Find the Receipt ID.
3. Tap **Issue Refund**.
4. Select "Full" or "Partial".
